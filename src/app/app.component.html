1. What is SlaynRookers?

Is an open source project (you can also contribute) with the aim of creating a community for the residents of Rookgaard in Slaynville.

We believe that a portal to integrate all rookgaard residents would be of great value. We see several possibilities for an open place to share adventures, make exchanges, stay up to date with news and much more.
